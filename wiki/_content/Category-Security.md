# Category:Security

[Users and groups](/index.php/Users_and_groups "Users and groups") are used on GNU/Linux for access control. The superuser (root) has complete access to the operating system and its configuration; it is intended for administrative use only. Unprivileged users can use the [su](/index.php/Su "Su") and [sudo](/index.php/Sudo "Sudo") programs for controlled privilege escalation.

This subject of [security](/index.php/Security "Security") includes a wide range of topics.
