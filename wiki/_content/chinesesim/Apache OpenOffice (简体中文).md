## Arch Linux中的OpenOffice

Arch Linux官方已经结束了对OpenOffice.org的支持，并选择了[LibreOffice (简体中文)](/index.php/LibreOffice_(%E7%AE%80%E4%BD%93%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87) "LibreOffice (简体中文)")。了解详情：[Dropping Oracle OpenOffice (arch-general)](https://mailman.archlinux.org/pipermail/arch-general/2011-March/018819.html).

推荐使用[LibreOffice (简体中文)](/index.php/LibreOffice_(%E7%AE%80%E4%BD%93%E4%B8%AD%E6%96%87) "LibreOffice (简体中文)")。
