**Minetest** is a free and open source voxel-based game engine. It was inspired by InfiniMiner and [Minecraft](/index.php/Minecraft "Minecraft"). The engine comes with a default game, but also allows for the use of other games referred to as sub-games.

## Installation

Minetest can be installed from [minetest](https://www.archlinux.org/packages/?name=minetest) or [minetest-git](https://aur.archlinux.org/packages/minetest-git/)<sup><small>AUR</small></sup>.
