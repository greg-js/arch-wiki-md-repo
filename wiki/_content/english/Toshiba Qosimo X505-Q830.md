### Audio

#### Internal Mic

To get the internal mic working, somewhere under /modprobe.d:

```
options snd-hda-intel model=dell-vostro

```

#### HDMI Audio

To get HDMI audio out working, per [This Arch forums thread](https://bbs.archlinux.org/viewtopic.php?id=90350): In /etc/modprobe.d/sound.conf:

```
 options snd-hda-intel probe_mask=0xffff,0xfff2

```

The HDMI audio ALSA device is then **plughw:1,3**, and can be tested like so:

```
 aplay -D plughw:1,3 /usr/share/sounds/alsa/Noise.wav

```

### Touchpad

Works, does not require additional drivers/software.

### Function keys

Some function keys work (when typed with with FN key):

*   ESC- Toggles muted on/off
*   F3- Hibernate (save state to disk and powers off)
*   F6- Brightness down
*   F7- Brightness up
*   F9- Toggle touchpad on/off

### Other special keys

*   The small button above the touchpad to toggle touchpad on/off works
*   The mute toggle on/off on the left side
*   The volume up/down buttons on the left side

### Webcam

Works out of the box, tested in [KDE](/index.php/KDE "KDE") and with [Skype](/index.php/Skype "Skype").

### USB

Works out of the box.

Note: When booting from USB, the drives change during boot. When prompted that the boot drive can not be found, remove and re-insert the USB stick.
